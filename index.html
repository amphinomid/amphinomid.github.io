Anne Li

I'm momentarily on leave from school to pursue reporting.

My campus paper, The Stanford Daily, is where I got my start and later served as a News managing editor and an Equity Project editor. I'm indebted to my first editors for believing in me.

After college I spent a summer reporting for Mission Local in San Francisco. The city took shape in my mind as I learned its contours by the Muni lines; people told me astonishing and delightful things when I least expected.

In a past life that feels distant now, I did coding olympiads and security research and got a bachelor's with honors in computer science. I had fun and learned a great deal except what to do with myself, and then I fell in love with stories.
