Anne Li


I'm a reporting intern at The Markup. I'm currently based in Berkeley, California.

***

I cut my teeth at The Daily and later edited its News section and Equity Project. Summer after undergrad I spent at Mission Local: San Francisco took shape in my mind as I learned its contours by the Muni lines; I found putting socks and shoes back on after running into the frigid ocean was not so bad and began to always do so; people told me astonishing and delightful things when I least expected.

Before falling in love with journalism, I did coding contests, software engineering and security research and got a bachelor's with honors in computer science, focusing on computer systems.
