I'm a journalist in Little Rock, Arkansas. Reach me at amphinomid at gmail dot com.

I cut my teeth at The Stanford Daily and later edited its News section and Equity Project; spent a summer flitting about San Francisco on the Muni, running into the frigid Pacific, learning astonishing and delightful things writing for Mission Local (supported by a Rebele grant). Then moved around and wrote more and did all the other things one does when twentysomething and going insane for the first time. In what feels like another life, I researched online safety and got a bachelor's with honors in computer science, focusing on systems.
